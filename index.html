About

PLHH met at Founders & Coders in February 2018.

We loved working together and formed a design agency built upon our core values and specialising in projects for the non-profit sector.

We can't wait to hear from you!

Accessible

Accessibility is a fundamental value of PLHH. We believe internet users should be able to access content no matter what their preferred device, their connection speed, or their use of assistive technologies.

Fast

We avoid using bloated frameworks and libraries and write lean code so that your sites and apps will run at lightning speed - any time, anywhere!

Secure

Since we don't know how to build you a backend yet, you won't have any data to be stolen!

Community

We will publish your code on the web... Perhaps you don't want that, but the community loves it. Open source facilitates best prectices for everybody.
